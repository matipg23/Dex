---

## What It Is

A visual org chart built from Dayforce (UK/US/Canada employees) and Slack (everyone else). Shows:
- **Tree view** — line management / reporting structure
- **Teams view** — team, vertical, and domain membership *(currently disabled pending reliable data source)*

---

## How to Use

- Open the live tool and search by name
- Click a node for details, Slack, email, or share links
- Navigation: click-drag to move, pinch/Cmd+scroll to zoom

---

## Notes for People Lookups

- UK/US/Canada employees: data from Dayforce (accurate)
- Others: data from Slack profiles (user-maintained, may be incomplete)
- If someone doesn't appear: message Aidan Robinson on Slack
- Not to be used for regulatory submissions (team view data is user-entered)

---

## When to Use This in Dex

- When building a new person profile and need to verify role, team, or manager
- When someone is mentioned in meetings without enough context to identify them
- When Matias mentions someone new and I need to place them in the org
